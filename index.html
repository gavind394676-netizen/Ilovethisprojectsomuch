About Me

Guitar teacher, lifelong musician

Hi! I'm a passionate guitarist based in Abbotsford. I picked up my first guitar at age 10 and never looked back — music quickly became the most important thing in my life, and I want to share that spark with the next generation.

The String Theory is my brand new venture, and I'm putting everything I have into making it great. My lessons are designed to be fun first — because kids learn best when they're enjoying themselves. I mix games, familiar songs, and progressive technique so every student builds real skills without even noticing how hard they're working.
